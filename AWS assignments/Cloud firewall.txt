Q1 - What are Outbound Rules?

Ans -Outbound means outgoing traffic from your EC2 instances. To connect internet or any browser you 
have to add outbound rule.

Q2 - What are Inbound Rules?

Ans -Inbound means incoming traffic coming to your EC2 instances. For that you have to add inbound 
rule.
